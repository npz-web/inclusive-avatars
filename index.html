Most character creators - even from big ticket games like the Sims 4 - neglect to accommodate to disabled players to grant them the right to see representations of themselves in the games they love. With the help of Shauna Keating and Kristoph Lalicki, this website can give a glimpse into what representation can be. This builder is based on the open-source character creator framework, hackrew(opens in a new window).
